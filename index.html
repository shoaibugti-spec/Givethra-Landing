Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about Givethra.

What is Givethra?

A humanitarian platform where verified people in need are connected with verified Heroes. Help goes directly to the school, hospital or utility company — not into unknown hands.

How does verification work?

Every user completes KYC. Every case requires documents (bill/challan), a live selfie and a video statement. Givethra reviews and verifies each case — including 1Bill reference checks — before it is published.

How do Heroes help?

Heroes unlock a verified case with Hero Credits, then either pay the institution directly using the verified reference number, or contribute any amount to the case's fundraising.

How is privacy protected?

Public visitors only see a limited preview (story, category, city, amount). Documents, bills, videos and payment references are visible only to verified Heroes after unlocking.

How do I submit a case?

Sign in at givethra.org, complete KYC, choose your category, select your company/institute, add your reference number and bill photo — your first case is completely FREE.

How do I become a Hero?

Sign in at givethra.org, add Hero Credits to your wallet (1 credit = unlock 1 case), and start helping verified families today.
